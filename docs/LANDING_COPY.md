## 5 FAQs

**Q1: Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit is completely free, requires no account, and your data never leaves your browser. We make money when teams upgrade to paid monitoring plans — not by selling your data.

**Q2: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
All estimates are based on published list pricing from each tool's website. We flag when an estimate has higher uncertainty (marked "rough confidence"). Actual savings depend on your contract terms and negotiated rates.

**Q3: Do you integrate with our billing systems?**
Not yet. You enter your spend manually — which takes about 5 minutes and forces you to actually look at what you're paying. Most teams find that exercise alone surfaces surprises.

**Q4: What AI tools do you support?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools are added based on user requests.

**Q5: What happens after the audit?**
You get a detailed report with findings ranked by savings impact, reasoning for each recommendation, and a shareable link. Enter your email to get the full report sent to your inbox with an AI-generated summary.
