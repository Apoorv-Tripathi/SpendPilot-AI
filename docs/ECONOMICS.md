# ECONOMICS.md — Unit Economics

## Estimated Lead Value

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| Avg monthly AI spend per team audited | $800 |
| Avg potential savings identified | $270/mo |
| Lead's willingness to pay for ongoing monitoring | $29–$99/mo |
| Estimated LTV (12-month plan, 60% retention) | $420 |

A team spending $800/month on AI tools and finding $270 in waste is highly motivated to pay $49/month to prevent it recurring. The ROI is obvious.

## CAC Assumptions

**Current stage (0 budget):**
- CAC = $0 (organic only)
- Cost = founder time (~3 hours per 10 users acquired)

**Paid stage ($5K/month budget):**
- LinkedIn ads to engineering leads: ~$80 CPL
- Content + SEO: ~$30 CPL blended over 6 months
- Target blended CAC: $45–$60

**Payback period at $49/mo plan:** ~1 month at $45 CAC.

## Conversion Funnel

```
Landing page visit
        ↓  (35% start audit)
Audit form filled
        ↓  (80% complete)
Results page viewed
        ↓  (25% enter email)
Lead captured
        ↓  (12% convert to paid)
Paying customer
```

**Benchmarks to hit:**
- Audit completion rate > 70%
- Email capture rate > 20%
- Lead → paid conversion > 10%

## Conditions Required to Reach $1M ARR

$1M ARR = ~$83K MRR = ~1,700 paying teams at $49/mo

**Required:**
1. 8,500+ audits completed per month (at 20% email capture, 10% conversion)
2. At least one viral distribution moment (PH launch, HN front page, tweet thread)
3. Annual plan option ($399/year) to improve cash flow
4. Churn below 5% monthly — product must save money visibly every month
5. Either: (a) self-serve SEO machine, or (b) one enterprise deal at $2K+/mo

**Realistic timeline:** 18–24 months post-launch with consistent execution.
